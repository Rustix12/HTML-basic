NVIDIA Corporation is an American multinational technology company that primarily focuses on designing and manufacturing graphics processing units (GPUs), as well as products for computing, gaming, and artificial intelligence (AI). It's one of the most influential and innovative tech companies in the world, particularly in the fields of graphics technology, gaming, and AI. Here’s an overview of the company:

Founding and Early History:

Founded: 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem.

Headquarters: Santa Clara, California, USA. NVIDIA initially started with a focus on developing high-performance graphics hardware for the growing PC gaming market, but over time it expanded its product offerings and technologies beyond just gaming. Key Milestones in NVIDIA’s Growth Graphics Cards and GPUs

1999: NVIDIA released its first graphics card, the RIVA TNT, which helped establish its presence in the gaming and graphics

2000s: NVIDIA began producing a series of successful graphics cards, including the GeForce line, which became widely used by gamers and content creators.
